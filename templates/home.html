Can you help Violet escape her nightmare?

Violet is stuck in her nightmare and must escape! But she needs your help to do it. Are you willing to take on the challenge and help Violet escape the ghosts that haunt her dreams?

Click here to play the game

How to play the game!

  1. Please register to play the game
  2. Use the arrows on your keyboard to move Violet
  3. Press space to shoot Violet's ghost
  4. You can only shoot one bullet at a time
  5. The grim reaper is chasing Violet, so make sure you dont let him catch you as you will lose a life
  6. You cannot kill the grim reaper, so move fast
  7. You have only three lives, so tread carefully
  8. Find the key to win
  9. Dont let any ghosts touch you or you will be repostioned to the start of the game and you will lose a life
  10. Collect powerups for a helping hand
  11. Collect coins to try and beat your high score!

How things work!

  1. The player can move anywhere except for the tiled areas as I've blocked them using a tile collison method
  2. The powerup will make you go quicker for a 5 seconds
  3. When you lose one life, you will return to your orginal position
  4. When you lose all lives, the game will stop
  5. Coins add to your score
  6. The key tells the game to stop and that you have won
  7. Press the reset button at any point of the game if you want to reset the game
  8. Press the leaderboard button if you want to see the leaderboard
  9. Only your high score will be saved to the leaderboard
